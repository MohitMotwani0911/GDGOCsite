ABOUT

GDSC IET-DAVV is a safe haven for tech geeks. Here, we are helping build a community that is welcoming to everyone irrespective of anything but their passion for learning technology.

We hold enriching discussions, productive talks, and hands-on workshops regularly to keep everyone up to date with bleeding-edge technology and trending topics in the industry. It is a community of tech-minded people in IET DAVV who experience what real-world collaborative software development is and allows everyone to pursue big, useful, and exciting projects that are not possible alone.

We have a community where we discuss new ideas regarding development and learn a lot while having fun.
